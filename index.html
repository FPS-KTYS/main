Program Overview

The Foundation in Physical Science is a pre-university program tailored for students who wish to pursue degrees in engineering,and other physical science fields. The program emphasizes analytical thinking, problem-solving, and a strong grasp of core scientific principles.

Core Subjects

  • Mathematics
  • Physics
  • Chemistry
  • Computing Fundamentals
  • Academic English
  • Scientific Communication

Admission Requirements

Applicants must have completed secondary education with strong performance in mathematics and science subjects. English language proficiency is also required.
